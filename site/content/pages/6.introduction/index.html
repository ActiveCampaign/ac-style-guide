What is Camp?

Camp is the global design language that drives ActiveCampaign. It encompasses everything visual. From product interfaces to our marketing writing. Camp is a foundation that we build upon to unify our products and services. Camp is maintained by the designers and front-end developers at ActiveCampaign.
