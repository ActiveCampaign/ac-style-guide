section_title6: Usage section_content6: |

Lead-in title, heading, body content and CTA

1 - Lead-in Title

Use a lead-in title to introduce and describe the topic of your accompanying copy. It always remains the same style.

2 - Main Heading

Headings can be used on their own but should be descriptive and powerful in their overall meaning. Should always be Poppins Bold.

3 - Body Copy

Body copy allows you to describe your topic from your heading or lead-in title. Default size is 14px.

4 - CTA

When speaking of a topic, always consider accompanying your copy with a CTA so visitors can learn more about your topic.

Small titles with body and CTA

1 - Title

Titles, much like their big sibling headings are used to describe the context of the following paragraph copy.

2 - Body Copy

The size of body copy should be smaller than the title. In this example the title is 18px and body is 16px.

3 - CTA

The CTA always allows the reader to read more on the topic just discussed. Use it as much as possible when discussing topics.
