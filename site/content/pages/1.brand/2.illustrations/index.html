Guidelines

Size

Icons come in three sizes: Small (32px), Medium (64px) and Large (128px) which coincide with our grid. As icons get bigger, they gain details and new colors.

 

Colors

Icons are primarily shades of our Ocean color palette. However, this can be changed depending on where the icon is being used. Also, an accent color is added at Medium and Large sizes.

Borders

Exterior borders are always 2px. Borders are primarily solid. Dashed borders can be used on occasion. All borders have a projecting cap.

 

Backgrounds

Optionally you can add a background to your spot illustration to make it stand out even more. Backgrounds are set circular designs that you can choose from.

Usages

Spot above content aligned left

 

Spot above content, center aligned

Spot aligned to the left of content

 

Spot inserted into a blank interface state. Add text to your spot to give context to the situation.
