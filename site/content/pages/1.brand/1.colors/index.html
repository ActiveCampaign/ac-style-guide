How to use Ocean

Ocean is our primary color and is used mostly for primary actions as well as for primary focal elements throughout our marketing material and products.

Primary action buttons

Information blocks and notices

Text links

Pills, tags and chips

Form element active states

How to use Strawberry

Strawberry plays an important role in providing visual feedback on destructive actions or items that require more attention or completion of the highest priority.

Destructive action buttons

Error blocks and notices

Chips that provide negative feedback

Form element error states

Negative trends

How to use Lavender

Lavender is used primarily as an accent color and provides no overall meaning. Use Lavender to accent elements like charts, or gems or other elements.

Gems and accent elements

Accent for charts

How to use Mint

Mint is used throughout our products as our main secondary color to compliment our blues. It is also used as a positive reinforcing color on things like success messages and validated form fields.

Success messages and notifications

Positive trends

Chips that provide positive feedback

Form element validation states

Mint as accent for charts

Gems and accent elements

How to use Creamsicle

Creamsicle is used primarily as an accent color and provides no overall meaning. Use Creamsicle to accent elements like charts, or gems or other elements.

Gems and accent elements

Accent for charts

How to use Banana

Banana is used primarily to display caution or to draw attention to elements that require more information but are low priority.

Warning messages and notifications

Chips that provide cautious feedback

Accent for charts
